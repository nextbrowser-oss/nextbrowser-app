# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want participation in this repository to be respectful, safe, and useful. Contributors and maintainers are expected to:

- communicate constructively and assume good intent;
- welcome different backgrounds, identities, and levels of experience;
- give specific, actionable feedback and accept it professionally;
- respect privacy, attribution, and project boundaries;
- focus disagreements on ideas and evidence rather than people.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, personal attacks, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, impersonation, spam, and publishing another person's private information without permission.

## Scope

This policy applies in issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other spaces where someone is representing this repository or its community.

## Reporting and enforcement

Do not open a public issue about a conduct incident. Contact the maintainer through the [repository owner's GitHub profile](https://github.com/nextbrowser-oss) and request a private channel. Include only the context needed to arrange private follow-up; do not publish sensitive details.

Maintainers may remove content or apply a correction, warning, temporary restriction, or permanent restriction according to the severity and pattern of behavior. Reports will be handled as privately as practical, with attention to the safety of affected participants.

## Attribution

This policy draws on the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
